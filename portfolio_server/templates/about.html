03 : About me

Intro

A graduate Astrophysicist, that decided to take a leap of faith and persue a career in Data Analytics/Science with the purpose of expanding my skills in mathematical analysis and programming. Some of the University projects I have worked on were:

  1. Gravitational Waves Data Analysis - Posterior Mass Determination Using Bayesian Statistics
  2. Binary Black Hole model Selection with Bayesian Statistics
  3. Literature Report - Dark Energy and Dark Matter

Career as a Data Analyst

During my 2 years as a data analyst, I successfully managed to develop and expand my skills as a Data Analyst. Assisted in developing dashboards and automated data mining processes, and with my team, we were the backbone of the effort of transitioning excel based databases to PostgreSQL Cloud hosted databases. Building the architecture from scratch and deploying various pipelines in order to safely migrate the excel tables in to our new Structured Database. Then finding and implementing third party tools , in order to design custom applications, that enabled non-technical analysts to perform CRUD operations to the Database, without having to type a single line of SQL!!! During the last two years, I was essential in the development of our company's tech stack, and developed skills in tools like GCP, AWS, Airflow, Kubernetes, Docker and Git Submodules.
